About Industry Salon

At Industry Salon, we believe great hair is only part of the experience, how you feel when you’re here matters just as much.

We created a space where artistry meets intention. From the moment you walk in, you’re invited to slow down, unwind, and make yourself at home.

Specializing in personalized color and precision cuts, every service is thoughtfully tailored to your features, lifestyle, and goals while maintaining our focus on healthy, beautiful hair.

With calming details and elevated touches throughout your visit, our purpose is simple: for you to leave feeling refreshed, confident, and truly cared for 🤍.
